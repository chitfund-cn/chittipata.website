About Chit Funds

Chit funds are a popular savings and borrowing scheme practiced in India and other countries. They operate as a type of rotating savings and credit association, where a group of people contributes a fixed amount of money every month. Each month, one member takes the collected sum through a bidding or lottery process.

How Chit Funds Work

  • A group (called a "chit group") is formed with a fixed number of members and a tenure.
  • Each member contributes a fixed amount every period (typically monthly).
  • Every period, a pooled amount is given to one member, either by auction or lottery.
  • The process continues until all members have received the pooled amount once.

About Chittipata.website
